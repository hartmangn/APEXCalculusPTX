Using the constant and quotient rules,

\begin{align*} \lim\limits_{x\to }\left(\frac{3f(x)}{g(x)}\right)\amp = \frac{\lim\limits_{x\to }(3f(x))}{\lim\limits_{x\to }g(x)}\\ \amp = \frac{3\lim\limits_{x\to }f(x)}{\lim\limits_{x\to }g(x)} \\ \amp \frac{3(6)}{3} = 6 \text{.} \end{align*}
