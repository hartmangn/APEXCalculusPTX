Assuming \(x\) is near \(1\text{,}\) \(2x+5\) is positive and we can drop the absolute value signs on the right.

\begin{gather*} \abs{x-1}\cdot\abs{2x+5} \lt \frac{\varepsilon}{2x+5}\cdot(2x+5)\\ \abs{2x^2+3x-5} \lt \varepsilon\\ \abs{(2x^2+3x+1) -6} \lt \varepsilon\text{,} \end{gather*}

which is what we wanted to prove.
