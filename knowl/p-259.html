Now we can apply the definition.

\begin{align*} \abs{x-3}\amp\lt\delta\\ \abs{x-3}\amp\lt\frac{\varepsilon}{7}\\ \abs{x-3}\amp\lt\frac{\varepsilon}{\abs{x+3}}\\ \abs{x-3}\cdot\abs{x+3}\amp\lt\varepsilon\\ \abs{x^2-9}\amp\lt\varepsilon\\ \abs{\left(x^2-3\right)-6}\amp\lt\varepsilon \end{align*}

In other words, \(\abs{x-3}\lt\delta\) implies \(\abs{\left(x^2-3\right)-6}\lt\varepsilon\text{.}\) This is what we needed to prove.
