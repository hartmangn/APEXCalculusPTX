If either of the following holds:

  1. \(\lim\limits_{x\to c}f(x)=L\text{,}\) \(\lim\limits_{x\to L}g(x)=K\text{,}\) and \(g(L)=K\)

  2. \(\lim\limits_{x\to c}f(x)=L\text{,}\) \(\lim\limits_{x\to L}g(x)=K\text{,}\) and \(f(x)\neq L\) for all \(x\) close to but not equal to \(c\)

then \(\lim\limits_{x\to c}g(f(x)) = K\text{.}\)
